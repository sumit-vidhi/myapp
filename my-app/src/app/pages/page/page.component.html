The single biggest reason people don’t reach their goals is MOTIVATION. Most people don’t have a natural motivation which is needed to continue a healthy lifestyle. The single biggest reason for people to reach their goals is social interaction.

Wellfaster supplies our customers with a large community of personal trainers, who will help you through the tough days. They will set up a home exercise programme for you, analyse your results and progress and get you back on track the motivation is low.

With the unique Virtual Training®-platform it’s possible to have your own personal trainer at an affordable price. This is your personal trainer in your pocket.
